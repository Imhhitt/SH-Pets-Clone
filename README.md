# ✨ Features that will make your server stand out

### 💫 Unleash Fully Customizable Pet Interactions
Bring your world to life with endless possibilities! Execute commands, messages, titles, and even custom actions every time a pet interacts. From greetings to special rewards — design unforgettable bonds between players and their loyal companions.

### 🧸 Choose from Custom Heads & Mobs
Let your creativity shine! Players can summon pets using custom heads or any Minecraft mob — including adorable baby versions! Give every pet a unique identity and personality that makes your server stand out instantly.

### ⚙️ Dynamic, Event-Based Triggers
Create truly interactive experiences. Link pet actions to server events like periodic timers, **player events (on hurt, interact, …)**, or special moments. Every event can come alive with animations, effects, or rewards — it’s all in your control.

### 🌈 Stunning Particle Effects & Animations
Turn ordinary pets into magical companions! Choose from dazzling particle trails, orbiting effects, bursts, or sparkles that follow each step. Every player will feel like their pet is part of a living fantasy world.

### 🪄 No-Code Setup, Total Power
Get started in minutes! Simple configuration files let you design complex pet behaviors with ease — no coding required. Focus on creativity while the plugin handles the hard work behind the scenes.

### 🎮 Boost Player Engagement & Retention
Players adore collecting and customizing pets — and this plugin turns that love into long-term retention. Create collectible series, cosmetic upgrades, and ability unlocks that keep your community playing and exploring for hours.

### 💬 Seamless Integration
Full **PlaceholderAPI** and **MiniMessage** support allows you to personalize names, messages, and visuals with gradients, hover effects, and clickable text. Give your server that modern, polished feel players expect.

### 🏆 Professional Support & Community-Driven Updates
Join hundreds of creative server owners who trust our plugins to enhance their worlds. Enjoy fast, friendly support and continuous updates shaped by your feedback. Your success is our mission.
